A little about me.

Hi! I'm Sarah, a punctuation pirate, word wrangler, and professional legal proofreader.

The best part of my job is helping people polish their hard work to ensure the integrity of the finished product. As a trained legal proofreader, I’m able to catch errors specific to legal transcripts so that you submit the most accurate representations to your clients.

Let me help your work reach the top.

What I do

I search for typographical and grammatical errors, punctuation accuracy and consistency, mistaken homophones, and incorrect spelling and consistancy.

Experience

Examinations Under Oath, depositions, hearings, medical transcripts, workers’ compensation claims, malpractice claims, expert testimony, and general litigation.

Method

I mark corrections directly onto the .pdf file using iAnnotate, and send you back the corrected pages unless the entire transcript is requested. Click here to read more about my method.
